PUZZLE INPUT: 
Represents moves of a game of rock, paper, scissors 
(represented by 'A', 'B', and 'C' for the opponent, and 'X', 'Y', 'Z' for me).
The opponents' moves are in column 1, and my moves are in column 2. 
Each 'round' of the game is a row in the input file.


SCORING:
Each shape has a score according to the guide below: 
Rock: 1
Paper: 2
Scissors: 3

From my POV, a win, loss, or draw is scored like this:
Win: 6
Draw: 3 
Loss: 0

My total score for a round is the score of the shape I chose (rock, paper, scissors), 
plus the score of the outcome of a round (win, lost, drew)


GOAL:
Find the total score that I would get if I made moves against my opponent, according to the puzzle
input
